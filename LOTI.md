# 📦 LOTI Worker v1.0.0
First release of the woprker app of LOTI for android .
## 🚀 Key Features
1. Socket.io Bridge : 
Establishes a persistent, low-latency connection to your local Dockerized backend.

2. Instant Connection : 
Instantly link your device to the LOTI server just by putting your local address where your backend is hosted (eg. http://10.51.51.103:51785 ) .

3. Native SMS Dispatch : 
Utilizes the Android SMS Manager to send real messages for OTP testing.

4. Live Status Logs :
View incoming job requests and delivery status directly on the mobile UI.

## 🛠️ Installation & Setup
#### ⏬Download: 
Download the LOTI.apk attached below.

#### 👇Install: 
Open the file on your Android device (ensure "Install from Unknown Sources" is enabled).

#### 🛑Permissions: 
Grant SMS and Phone State permissions when prompted to allow the app to interact with the cellular hardware.

#### 🔗Connect:
 Ensure your phone is on the same Wi-Fi as your development machine, open the app, and scan the QR code from your dashboard.

## ⚠️ Requirements
Android OS: 8.0 (Oreo) or higher.

Network: Local Wi-Fi connectivity to the host machine.

Hardware: A device with an active SIM card and SMS plan.
